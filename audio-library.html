Raja Yoga Murli Audio & PDF Library

Welcome to the Raja Yoga Murli Audio & PDF Library, a single destination for listening to daily Murli classes and reading the corresponding PDF files in multiple international languages.

The library currently supports twenty-nine languages, enabling Raja Yoga students from different parts of the world to access spiritual knowledge in their preferred language.

By default, the page opens with Tamil (Chennai) selected, today's date automatically filled in, and the Listen Murli option enabled. You may change the language, choose any date, and either listen to the Murli audio or read the PDF file.

Features

🌍 29 International Languages

Access Murli audio and PDF files in twenty-nine languages from Assamese to Thai.

🎧 Daily Murli Audio

Listen to the daily Murli directly from your browser without downloading any additional software.

📖 PDF Reader

Open and read Murli PDF files for any available date.

📅 Automatic Date Selection

Today's date is selected automatically to help you access the latest Murli instantly.

Murli Selector

Whether you wish to listen to the Murli in Tamil, Hindi, English, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Sinhala, German, Thai, or any other supported language, this library provides a convenient and unified platform for accessing spiritual knowledge every day.
