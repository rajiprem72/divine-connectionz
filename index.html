Welcome to Divine Connectionz

Raja Yoga Meditation is a spiritual practice that helps us experience our true identity as an eternal soul and develop a loving relationship with the Supreme Soul, Paramathma. Although known by different names such as Shiva, Allah, God and Jehovah, the Supreme is understood to be the eternal source of peace, love and wisdom. Raja Yoga teaches that all human beings are souls and members of one universal family.

Why Divine Connectionz?

Divine Connectionz has been created for everyone who wishes to learn Raja Yoga Meditation in a simple, practical and systematic manner. Through spiritual articles, guided meditation, podcasts, videos and study resources, our mission is to help every soul experience peace, happiness and spiritual wisdom.

Understanding Raja Yoga

Raja Yoga is taught by Paramathma, the Supreme Father of all souls. The practice begins with soul consciousness—recognising ourselves as peaceful spiritual beings—and remembering the Supreme Soul. This loving remembrance, known as Manmana Bhava, quietens the mind and fills the soul with divine virtues.

Mindfulness & Inner Peace

Raja Yoga teaches us to observe our thoughts with awareness and consciously choose positive, elevated thinking. This practice helps reduce stress, improves emotional stability and develops lasting inner peace.

Self-Realisation & Spiritual Growth

Self-realisation is the foundation of Raja Yoga. As we deepen our connection with Paramathma, we naturally experience greater clarity, confidence, compassion and purpose in every aspect of life.

Begin Your Spiritual Journey

Whether you are beginning your meditation journey or have been practising for many years, Divine Connectionz offers resources that will inspire and support your spiritual growth. We invite you to explore our courses, podcasts, videos and articles, and experience the joy of Raja Yoga Meditation.
