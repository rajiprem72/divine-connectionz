Welcome to Divine Connectionz

Raja Yoga Meditation is a spiritual practice that helps us experience our true identity as an eternal soul and develop a loving relationship with the Supreme Soul, Paramathma. Although known by different names such as Shiva, Allah, God and Jehovah, the Supreme is understood to be the eternal source of peace, love and wisdom. Raja Yoga teaches that all human beings are souls and members of one universal family.

Why Divine Connectionz?

Divine Connectionz is created for everyone who wishes to learn and practise Raja Yoga Meditation in a simple and systematic way. Through articles, videos, audio lessons, podcasts and spiritual resources, the website aims to help seekers deepen their understanding and experience of Raja Yoga.

Understanding Raja Yoga

Raja Yoga is taught by Paramathma, the Supreme Father of all souls. The practice begins with soul consciousness—recognising ourselves as peaceful, spiritual beings—and remembering the Supreme Soul. This loving remembrance, known as Manmana Bhava, helps quieten the mind and leads to deep inner meditation.

Mindfulness and Inner Peace

Raja Yoga encourages mindful awareness of our thoughts, words and actions. By observing our thoughts without attachment and replacing negativity with elevated thinking, we gradually experience calmness, emotional balance and freedom from stress.

Self-Realisation and Spiritual Growth

Self-realisation is the heart of Raja Yoga. As we understand ourselves as souls and strengthen our connection with Paramathma, we develop inner strength, clarity and purpose. This spiritual awareness transforms relationships and daily life.

Begin Your Journey

Whether you are new to meditation or have been practising for many years, Divine Connectionz offers resources to support your spiritual journey. We invite you to explore, learn and experience the peace that comes through Raja Yoga Meditation.
