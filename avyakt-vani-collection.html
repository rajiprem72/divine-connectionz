Avyakt Vani Collection (Revision Course) – 1969 to 2017

From 1969 to 2017, Paramathma, along with Brahma, continued to teach Raja Yoga by entering the body of Dadi Gulzar. This phase of teaching is known as "Avyakt Vani".
