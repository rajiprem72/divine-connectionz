📱 Shiv Gyan – Murli App

Shiv Gyan is a comprehensive spiritual mobile application created for Raja Yoga students around the world. The app brings together Murli audio collections, Raja Yoga courses, spiritual calendars, traffic control songs, books and many valuable resources in a single place.

🌍 Sakar Vani Collection in 23 International Languages

The application contains the complete Sakar Vani collection in twenty-three international languages, ranging from Assamese to Thai.

To keep the application lightweight and relevant, only the latest six years of Murli files are maintained at any given time.

For example, during 2026, the app contains Vani files for:

  • 2020
  • 2021
  • 2022
  • 2023
  • 2024
  • 2025
  • 2026

In 2027, the oldest year will automatically be replaced with the newest collection, ensuring that users always have access to the latest six years of Murli classes.
