Sakar Vani Collection

The Sakar Vani (Murli) contains the divine knowledge spoken by Paramathma Shiva through the corporeal medium Shri Lekhraj Kripalani, whom Paramathma named Brahma Baba, between the years 1936 and 1969.

These invaluable spiritual teachings are revised and studied every day by Raja Yogis across the world. This collection provides downloadable archives in multiple languages, covering different periods.
