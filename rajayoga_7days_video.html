7 Days Raja Yoga Course in Tamil – Videos

Welcome to the complete 7 Days Raja Yoga Meditation Course in Tamil. This course is specially designed for beginners who wish to understand Raja Yoga Meditation in a simple, systematic and practical manner.

Over the years, thousands of brothers and sisters across the world have benefited from these classes. We pray that this course also helps you experience peace, happiness and a loving relationship with the Supreme Soul, Paramathma.

About This Course

The Raja Yoga 7-Day Course conducted by our team consists of 16 classes, each lasting approximately 30 minutes. The first fifteen classes explain the complete philosophy of Raja Yoga Meditation including the Soul, God, World Cycle, Karma, Human Births, Shri Krishna, Ravana, Meditation and many other important spiritual concepts.

The final class is a practical meditation session that teaches participants how to practice Raja Yoga Meditation in their everyday lives.

These classes were originally conducted online for enthusiastic European brothers and sisters during the Covid pandemic. The objective was to share spiritual knowledge and help every individual experience Inner Peace, Self Realisation, Mindfulness, Positive Thinking, Soul Consciousness and ultimately develop a loving relationship with the Supreme Soul.

How These Videos Were Created

Initially, the complete course was recorded only as audio. Later, three dedicated young Raja Yogis from Salem, one young brother and two young sisters, volunteered to convert the entire course into beautiful educational videos.

They carefully selected thousands of suitable images, synchronized them with the audio recordings, and spent nearly one full year creating all sixteen videos.

We sincerely appreciate their loving service and dedication. May the Supreme Soul continue to bless them abundantly.
