Raja Yoga meditation is a form of meditation that helps practitioners achieve a state of mental clarity and inner peace. It involves techniques such as visualization and concentration to focus the mind and attain a state of relaxation and awareness.

By sharing our Yennam Pol Vazhvu videos on various social media platforms and messaging apps, you are helping to promote the practice of Raja Yoga meditation to a wider audience.
